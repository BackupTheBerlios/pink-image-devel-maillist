Hello all;

Thanks for the great work on Pink;

I have found that pink.cpp.float2byte(float_image) silently converts the
image range to 0-255 irrespective of the content of the image

example:

In [20]: mybinn = pink.cpp.convert2float(mybin)

In [21]: pink.cpp.minmax(mybinn)
Out[21]: [0.0, 1.0]

In [22]: mybinb = pink.cpp.float2byte(mybinn)

In [23]: pink.cpp.minmax(mybinb)
Out[23]: [0, 255]


I believe it should be an option.

Cheers
