Hello all,

Eventually found a solution to my problem posted before, So what I've done:

1- test for the presence of Boost_SYSTEM_LIBRARY (libboost_system) in the main CMakeList.txt and do all the associated stuff (add to LIBS, etc)
2- before the test for boost thread, add this library to CMAKE_CXX_FLAGS (this is a heresy in my opinion, feel free to propose something else)
3- after the final boost tests, remove this library from CMAKE_CXX_FLAGS (otherwise warnings in the last 20% of the compilation of pink because there is a linking directive in the compilation command)
4- add Boost_SYSTEM_LIBRARY to the correct LIBS in src/lib/CMakeList.txt (this took me a long time to find out)

I think that's it. It is relatively simple but for me this is a hack.

Eventually I removed all the experiments I made in the UJ_xxx files. I hope I didn't break anything. It is in mercurial and Laszlo is testing it.

This has taken me several hours of trial and errors.

Cheers to all.
