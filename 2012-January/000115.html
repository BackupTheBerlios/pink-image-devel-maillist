Hello, 

in Boost 1.48 they have changed the nomenclature of the template classes. They 
call them RT0, RT1, ... However RT1 was a constant in the fft function. I have 
changed the constant name to PINK_RT1. Now Pink should compile with both the 
older and newer boosts. The update should be available from r693 which should 
be uploaded by the time You wake up.
