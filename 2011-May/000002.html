Hello Everybody,

for the increasing number of discussion and issue reports, as a next step of 
Pink's open development, I have started to unify Pink's communication 
channels. This starts with the setup of a mailing list for discussing possible 
issues and design choices. Everybody interested in Pink can view and, at her 
will, participate in the discussion related to usage, development and new 
feature discussions. For those who are already involved, please keep posting 
issues. 

You can post without registration to pink-image-devel at lists.berlios.de

To see all the mails or receive digests, you can subscribe at 
https://lists.berlios.de/mailman/listinfo/pink-image-devel

You also have a bug-tracker at your disposition at
http://developer.berlios.de/bugs/?group_id=12429
you need to register to the bug-tracker, but only a valid email address is 
needed. The registration is automatic and does not need a phone number or 
further approval.

Furthermore, we are envisaging to move the source code currently held on a 
private svn server to a public mercurial repository. This way you will be able 
to post patches or improvement. 


happy coding, 

ujoimro
